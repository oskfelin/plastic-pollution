THE GREAT PACIFIC GARBAGE PATCH
The Great Pacific Garbage Patch is the largest accumulation
of ocean plastic in the world and is located between Hawaii
and California. Scientists of The Ocean Cleanup have conducted
the most extensive analysis ever of this area.
